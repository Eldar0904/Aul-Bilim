What we do

Three ways we change a child's odds

From equipping classrooms to guiding orphaned teenagers into careers — every programme meets a village where it is.

School Fit-Out

We furnish and equip rural classrooms from scratch — modern desks, science labs, digital tools, and everything a great lesson needs.

Learn more →

Teacher Training

Through USTAZ Coworking centres, rural teachers join workshops, mentoring, and professional communities — closing the gap with city educators.

Learn more →

Child Mentorship

Our Samruk Umiti programme guides teenagers from orphanages through career choices, college applications, and their first steps into adult life.

Learn more →

Why a classroom changes everything

A rural school may have one teacher covering five subjects, with furniture from the 1990s. A child's environment shapes what they believe they can become — so we rebuild it, together with the people who live there.
